Apply to be a part of our club!

Minimun Criteria to Apply for Club:

  • Applicant must be a student of VPM's B.N. Bandodkar College of Science.
  • Applicant must be enrolled either in a Undergraduate or Post-Graduate course.
  • Applicant from any stream can apply for program.
  • Applicant should attend monthly conferences once they are a part of club.
  • Promotion of member will be based on project submissions.
  • Applicant must devote sometime for Club activities in order to get the most out of it.

If you fit in above criteria & have a genuine interest in technology, make sure to apply & be a part of this magnificent & growth-driven community.
